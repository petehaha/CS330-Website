Photography Project

I'm part of the Photography Club at Northwestern, and this past February, we took a trip to Wicker Park in Chicago to have a photoshoot. It was my first time exploring the surburbs outside of Evanston, and I had a blast!

We had a total of 5 people come that day. We got to see really cool murals as well as take shots from many different angles. I hope to go visit other cool surburbs around Chicago next year!
